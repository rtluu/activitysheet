Being a product manager is my true role. I love driving products from concept to reality by connecting business, development, and design.

But defining my role as a product manager can be difficult at times because every circumstance need something different. I am constantly having to adapt to the needs of my product, market, and teammates while balancing both budget and time.

So I spent some time today researching more about how other people define the role of a product manager and the techniques they use to optimize their effectiveness.

I found a lot of stuff, but one thing that I really like is this graphic.

It does a very good job of breaking down the areas a PM is involved with and the responsibilities within each area of focus.
