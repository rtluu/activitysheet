I realized that I got ahead of myself with my first crack at my personal site.

Osebo suggested that I take more time to think through the purpose of my personal site and to sketch multiple iterations before touching the computer.

My goals with my personal site are to:

  • Showcase my web work through individual case studies.
  • Convey my multi-disciplinary background and experience.
  • Capture viewers attention and entice them to contact me.
  • Keep things minimal while adding my own personal flare.
  • Use a new Javascript library to create a unique interactive.

I also think I'm going to build my personal site in gulp.js. I've never used it before, but I know it's fast and I've heard good things.
