--- layout: post title: "Footer update for all pages" date: 2017-03-06 categories: jekyll update ---

The contact section on my homepage served as a footer, but it didn't feel like a footer and it wasn't practical to use on every page of my site. I remade the contact section into a universal footer to achieve a consistent experience.

It is mobile friendly as well with the text links transforming into icons at the 600px breakpoint.
