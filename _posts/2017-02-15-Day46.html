I had a great conversation today with an old friend about her problems collecting content at work. She's works in government and spends a lot of time researching politicians collecting articles, videos and more.

She said that she's been using sticky notes to collect links and write notes, but that is has gotten extremely unorganized and overwhelming.

The conclusion of the conversation was that she needed a solution that she equated to "Pinterest for the workplace." Once she said it, it was immeadiately evident that that was the niche we need to pursue.

I suggested for her to try the beta and will be following up with her and her team to learn more about how we can improve their collection and collaboration.
