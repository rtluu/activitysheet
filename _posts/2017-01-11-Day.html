Being a product manager is my true role. I love driving products from concept to reality by connecting business, development, and design.

But defining my role as a product manager can be difficult at times because every circumstance need something different. I am constantly having to adapt to the needs of the product, market, and teammates while balancing both budget and time.

So I spent some time today researching more about how other people define the role of a product manager and the techniques they use to optimize their effectiveness.

I found a lot of stuff, but one that I really like is this diagram.

When thinking about building products, all aspects need to be considered and if one of the circles is unmet, the product is bound to fail.

I'm excited to learn more and to start applying for roles as a PM.
