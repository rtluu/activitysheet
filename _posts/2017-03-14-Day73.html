The north east got hit by a mix of snow/sleet last night. I woke up to a snowy driveway and a competitor on Designer News called Buckets.

They are a visual bookmarking tool that take screenshots of urls. It's built in wordpress and got a decent amount of upvotes on Product Hunt.

It's definitely a good sign and shows that users continue to face a problem when trying to collect content online. The team and I are learning a lot from analyzing the product and the comments that they've received.
